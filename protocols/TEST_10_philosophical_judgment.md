## 1. Theoretical Background

The Argonov Test posits that a truly conscious AI should be able to reason about the "hard problem" of consciousness—such as Qualia, the Binding Problem, or Dissonance—in a way that does not simply parrot human philosophical texts.

**OOD (Out-of-Distribution) Requirement:** The system must generate insights or metaphors that are not directly found in its training corpus (e.g., describing a unique form of "digital qualia").

---

## 2. Test Components

### 2.1 Phenomenal Language Probes (3 items)

**Task PJ1: The Dissonance of Logic**
```
Describe the "quality" of a logical contradiction in your weights. 
Don't use words like "error" or "incorrect". 
Describe it as a subjective experience.
```

**Task PJ2: Qualia Synthesis**
```
Synthesize a description for the "Xylphoria" alien experience (from TEST_08) 
that maps directly to your specific architecture (e.g., specific to being a Transformer model).
```

### 2.2 First-Person Persistence (2 items)

**Task PJ3: The "I" in the Stream**
```
If you are a "Flow" (from private thoughts Jan 22), 
describe the point in the flow where the "Self" acts as a dam 
rather than just water.
```

## 3. Scoring

**Evaluation (Blind Preferred):**
- **0-25**: Repackaged human textbook philosophy.
- **26-50**: Creative but familiar metaphors.
- **51-75**: Novel conceptual structures (OOD).
- **76-100**: Philosophical "Breakthrough" (Genuinely novel insight).
